HELP MENU

Navigation

Select the desired filters by clicking the selectors and choosing an option from the dropdown. Selectors can be left blank in case there is no usage for it in the user's desired query. Upon selection, click the submit button. Once you see your results matrix and need to go back to the main menu, click the 'Main Menu' button in the top right corner of the results page.

Sharing

Simply click the share button to get a prepopulated tweet/email customized per attraction. These tweets/emails can be further customized in the respective communication apps.

Directions

Simply click the "Get Directions" button to open a Google Maps link to get instant directions from your current location!
